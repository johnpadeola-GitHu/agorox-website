Do I need prior LabOS experience to enroll?
No. The program starts from LabOS navigation fundamentals and builds up to full administrative competency — no prior experience is required.
Is the certification recognized by employers?
Yes. The CLA is an AgoroX-issued, verifiable credential that demonstrates LabOS competency to current or prospective employers.
Can my organization enroll multiple staff members?
Yes. The Corporate Training option supports cohort-based enrollment, delivered onsite or virtually, for laboratories adopting LabOS at scale.
What's the difference between Online and Classroom delivery?
Online delivery is self-paced and remote. Classroom delivery is instructor-led, either in person or via live virtual sessions, and follows the same 2-day program structure.

PoultrySuite Certifications

PoultrySuite Africa is three independently-licensed systems — PoultryOS, HatcheryOS, and FeedMillOS. Certification follows the same shape: one foundational certification, one focused certification per product, and a combined credential for farms running all three.

Register Interest → All Programs
Why It's Structured This Way

You only learn what your farm actually runs.

A farm may license PoultryOS only, any two modules, or all three. A PoultryOS-only farm should never be required to learn Hatchery or Feed Mill operations it doesn't use — so each product has its own certification, and the combined Farm Manager credential is only required for farms (and staff) operating the full suite.

Certified PoultrySuite Farm Manager (CPFM)

Requires: CPO + CHO + CFO, each independently earned

No new modules of its own. CPFM certifies that a candidate can manage a farm running the complete suite, and can reason across systems — how a HatcheryOS hatch becomes a new PoultryOS batch, how a FeedMillOS delivery affects that batch's feed-conversion cost. Assessed entirely through one cross-system capstone scenario.

Receive a hatch. Take HatcheryOS output and create the resulting PoultryOS batch.
Record a feed delivery. Log a FeedMillOS production run and dispatch to that batch.
Report combined performance. Produce one view referencing cost data from FeedMillOS and origin data from HatcheryOS.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

My farm only uses PoultryOS — do I need the other certifications?
No. PoultrySuite Essentials plus CPO is a complete, standalone certification. HatcheryOS and FeedMillOS certifications are only relevant if your farm runs those modules.
Can I work toward CPFM if I don't hold all three certifications yet?
CPFM eligibility requires CPO, CHO, and CFO to all be complete first — they can be earned in any order, but CPFM is only unlocked once all three are held.
Is there a separate knowledge exam for CPFM?
No. Each prerequisite certification already includes its own knowledge assessment. CPFM is assessed entirely through the cross-system capstone scenario.
Can my organization enroll multiple farm managers?
Yes. The Corporate Training option supports cohort-based enrollment for agribusiness groups and multi-farm operators, on any combination of tracks.
